# Course Information Summary

## Project Metadata

- **Project Name:** NPU NextGen
- **Course Title (TH):** นักจัดการฟาร์มเกษตรแบบผสมผสานอัจฉริยะ
- **Course Title (EN):** Smart Integrated Farm Manager
- **Status:** Development / Recruitment Phase

## Program Details

- **Type:** Non-Degree Certificate Program
- **Duration:** 16–20 weeks (approx. 4–6 months)
- **Total Hours:** 285 Hours
  - Theory: 45 Hours
  - Practical/Work-Based: 240 Hours
- **Credits:** Equivalent to 9 Academic Credits
- **Target Capacity:** 40 students per batch (Total 80 for 2 batches)

## Key Learning Modules

1. **Module 1:** AI-Powered Analysis & Planning for Smart Farming
2. **Module 2:** IoT Implementation & Control in Farm Environments
3. **Module 3:** AI-Commerce, Digital Branding & Modern Sales

## Organizing Body

- **Lead Organizer:** Nakhon Phanom University (NPU)
- **Partners:**
  - Department of Agriculture (DOA)
  - Ministry of Commerce (MOC)
  - Young Smart Farmer (YSF) Network
  - SITC/IoTES Research Center
